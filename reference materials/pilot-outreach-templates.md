## Objection Handling Guide

**Objection**: "We're happy with how things work now."  
**Response**: "That's great! Out of curiosity, how much time per week would you say the board spends on administrative tasks like answering member questions, tracking maintenance, finding documents? [Wait for answer] And is that time you'd rather spend on strategic issues or other parts of your life?"

**Objection**: "We can't afford it."  
**Response**: "I totally understand budget constraints. The pilot is $200/month, and most co-ops find they save about 7 hours/week in volunteer time. At even $20/hour, that's $7,280/year in value for a $2,400/year cost. But I get it — let me know if your budget situation changes. I'm also happy to explore payment plans or deferred start dates."

**Objection**: "Our board members aren't tech-savvy."  
**Response**: "That's actually why I designed it this way — my target user is a 65-year-old volunteer board member who's comfortable with email but not much else. If you can use Gmail or check Facebook, you can use this. We also provide training and support. Want to see how simple the interface is?"

**Objection**: "We tried property management software before and hated it."  
**Response**: "Yeah, that's the problem — property management software is built for landlords, not member-governed co-ops. It doesn't understand committees, AGMs, bylaws, occupancy agreements, or how co-ops actually work. This is built from the ground up for co-ops. What specifically didn't work about the previous software?"

**Objection**: "We need to discuss this with the board."  
**Response**: "Absolutely, this is a board decision. Would it be helpful if I came to your next board meeting to present? I can do 20 minutes + Q&A. Or I can send you materials to share with the board — what would work better?"

**Objection**: "What if we sign up and don't like it?"  
**Response**: "It's month-to-month after the 3-month pilot. If it's not working, just let me know and you can cancel. You can also export all your data at any time. No long-term commitment, no penalties."

**Objection**: "We have a customized system that works for us."  
**Response**: "Interesting! What does your system look like? [Listen] That's actually pretty sophisticated. Out of curiosity, what happens when the person who built that system leaves the board? And is it accessible to all members, or just the board?"

**Objection**: "We're worried about data privacy."  
**Response**: "Great question. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, we're PIPA-compliant, and you own 100% of your data. We never sell or share member information. Role-based access controls mean members only see what they should. We also do regular security audits. Want me to send you the technical details?"
