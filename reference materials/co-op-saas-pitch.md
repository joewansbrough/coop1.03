# Co-op Housing Platform — Sales Pitch
## Modern software built for member-governed housing

---

## The Problem

**BC housing cooperatives are running critical governance operations with tools designed for landlords — or worse, spreadsheets and paper.**

Current property management software assumes a profit-driven landlord-tenant model. Co-ops are different:
- **Member-governed**, not owner-managed
- **Democratic decision-making** through committees, board meetings, AGMs
- **Shared responsibility** for maintenance and community wellbeing
- **Complex compliance** with the Co-operative Association Act and CHF BC guidelines
- **Volunteer administrators** juggling governance, communications, and operations

Most co-ops are stuck using:
- Email threads for member communications
- Shared Google Drives with inconsistent file naming
- Excel spreadsheets for maintenance tracking
- Physical binders for meeting minutes and policy documents
- Manual processes for waitlist management
- No institutional memory when board members turn over

**The result?** Administrative burnout, governance gaps, maintenance delays, and member frustration.

---

## The Solution

A **purpose-built SaaS platform designed exclusively for BC housing cooperatives** that understands:
- How co-ops actually work
- The BC regulatory environment (CAA, PIPA, CHF BC standards)
- The challenges of volunteer governance
- The importance of institutional knowledge

### Core Platform Features

**1. Member & Unit Management**
- Complete member directory with roles, committee assignments, and contact info
- Unit registry with occupancy history and turnover tracking
- Automated move-in/move-out workflows
- Waitlist management with transparent priority tracking
- Member onboarding checklists

**2. Maintenance Intelligence**
- Member-submitted maintenance requests with automatic routing
- Priority assessment and responsibility determination (member vs. co-op)
- Unit-level maintenance history and pattern detection
- Expense tracking per request
- Mobile-optimized submission and status checking

**3. Document & Knowledge Management**
- Centralized document library with category tagging
- Committee-specific document folders
- Search and retrieval across all governance documents
- Version control for policies and meeting minutes
- Secure access controls (public vs. board-only documents)

**4. Governance & Communications**
- Committee management with membership tracking
- Announcement system with priority levels
- Calendar for board meetings, AGMs, community events
- Email notifications and reminders
- Communications history and audit trail

**5. AI-Powered Policy Assistant**
- Natural language Q&A grounded in co-op documents
- Instant answers to member questions about rules, policies, bylaws
- Powered by BC baseline documents (CAA, CHF BC templates, Model Rules)
- Custom-tailored with your co-op's specific policies and minutes
- Always cites source documents for transparency

**6. Reports & Analytics**
- Occupancy rates and turnover analysis
- Maintenance cost tracking by unit and category
- Committee activity reporting
- Member participation metrics
- Export to Excel/PDF for board meetings

The platform is designed for volunteers with basic smartphone/email skills. If you can use Gmail or Facebook, you can use this. We provide training and support.

**Q: Can we keep using our existing accounting software?**
Yes. This platform handles governance and operations, not accounting. We can integrate with common accounting packages or you can export data for manual entry.

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're PIPA-compliant. Role-based access controls ensure members only see what they should. Regular security audits.

**Q: What if we need a custom feature?**
