# Co-op Housing Platform — Executive Summary

## Modern Governance Software for BC Housing Cooperatives

---

### The Problem
BC housing co-ops are managing complex governance operations with tools designed for landlords — or worse, spreadsheets and email threads. The result: administrative burnout, governance gaps, maintenance delays, and lost institutional knowledge when board members turn over.

### The Solution
A **purpose-built SaaS platform** designed exclusively for BC housing cooperatives that centralizes:
- Member & unit management with automated turnover workflows
- Maintenance intelligence with priority assessment and pattern detection
- Document management with AI-powered policy Q&A
- Governance tools for committees, communications, and events
- Reports and analytics for better board decisions

### Key Differentiators
1. **Built for Co-ops** — Understands member-governance, not landlord-tenant models
2. **BC-Specific** — Pre-loaded with CAA, CHF BC templates, Model Rules
3. **AI-Assisted** — Natural language policy assistant grounded in your documents
4. **Volunteer-Friendly** — Clean interface, mobile-optimized, no training required
5. **Data Sovereignty** — You own your data, export anytime, no lock-in

### Value Proposition
**Reclaim ~7 hours/week** in volunteer administrator time through automation and centralization. That's **$10,000+/year** in value (at $30/hour equivalent) for a **$2,400/year** investment (pilot pricing).
